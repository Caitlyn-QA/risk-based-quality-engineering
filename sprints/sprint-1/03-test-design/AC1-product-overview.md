# AC1 – Product Overview Test Design

## Purpose

Define the verification approach for the product overview based on the business requirements and investigation findings.

## Test Objective

Verify that visitors can successfully view and browse the available products from the home page.

## Test Conditions

Verify that:

- the product overview is displayed;
- all available products are shown;
- each product displays the required information;
- visitors can clearly distinguish individual products;
- selecting a product opens the correct product detail page.

## Product Risks Covered

- Visitors cannot discover available products.
- Product information is incomplete or unclear.
- Navigation to product details is unavailable.
- The product overview does not match the expected user experience.

## Automation Consideration

This functionality is a strong candidate for automation because it is business-critical, stable and likely to be executed frequently as part of regression testing.
