# Sprint 1 Overview

## Sprint Goal

Establish the first usable version of the product catalogue so visitors can discover available products, view detailed product information, browse by category and contact the business with questions or support enquiries.

## Important Changes

Sprint 1 introduces the first customer-facing version of the website.

Visitors can:

- browse available products and see each product’s name and price;
- open a product page to view more detailed information;
- view related products and navigate to their product pages;
- browse products belonging to a selected category;
- submit a message through the contact form.

## Quality Focus

The main quality focus for this sprint is to understand whether the first catalogue experience supports visitors in discovering products and continuing their shopping journey.
