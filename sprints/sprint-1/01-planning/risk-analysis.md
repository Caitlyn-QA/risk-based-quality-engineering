# Sprint 1 Risk Analysis

## Purpose

This document captures the main product risks identified before detailed testing begins.

The goal is to focus testing effort on areas where failure would have the greatest impact on visitors or the business.

## Product Overview Risk

Visitors may not be able to clearly understand which products are available.

If the product overview is missing, incomplete or difficult to use, visitors may lose confidence in the shop or leave before opening a product page.

## Product Detail Risk

Visitors may not receive enough information to decide whether a product is relevant.

If product details are missing, unclear or inconsistent with the overview, visitors may not trust the catalogue.

## Related Products Risk

Visitors may not be able to continue browsing from a product page.

If related products are missing or do not navigate correctly, the product journey may end too early.

## Category Browsing Risk

Visitors may not be able to browse products by category.

If category filtering behaves incorrectly, visitors may miss relevant products or see products that do not match their selected category.

## Contact Form Risk

Visitors may not be able to contact the business.

If the contact form fails or gives unclear feedback, support and sales enquiries may be lost.
