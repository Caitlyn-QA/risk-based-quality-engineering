# Product Overview Test Scenarios

## Purpose

Record the Sprint 1 test scenario decision for the Product Overview feature.

---

## Automation Decision

Product Overview automation is deferred for Sprint 1.

The current implementation is temporary and will be replaced by the planned card-based layout in a future sprint.

---

## Scenario Decision

No automated test scenarios will be created for Product Overview in Sprint 1.

---

## Rationale

Automated scenarios for the current list-based implementation would have limited value because they would likely need to be rewritten when:

- product cards replace list items
- product images are introduced
- the grid layout is implemented
- the clickable product element changes

---

## Future Scenario Ideas

When the card-based Product Overview is implemented, consider scenarios such as:

- Product cards are displayed in a grid.
- Each product card shows an image, name, and price.
- Selecting a product card opens the correct Product Detail page.
